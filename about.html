Our Philosophy

Quality Over Quantity.
Three Lines. No Compromise.

We don't supply fifteen articles. We supply three — and we have refined each one until there is nothing left to improve. Every order that leaves our looms meets the same specification as the first.

Our buyers restock on a schedule because they know exactly what they are getting. That predictability is worth more than a wide catalogue of inconsistent qualities.

Nova Silk — Flagship

How We Operate

Four Principles, No Exceptions

Consistency

Every thaan leaves Faisalabad to the same specification — weight, count, and finish — that was quoted. No surprises at delivery.

Reliability

Quotation within one business day. Restock cycles you can build a procurement schedule around. No unexplained delays.

Direct Trade

Manufacturer to buyer. No agent, no middleman, no markup layer between our looms and your warehouse. The price you get is the production price.

Traceability

Loom-to-delivery tracking. Yarn certifications from our suppliers available on request. Every roll can be accounted for.

Production

Faisalabad — Sole Production Hub

All ENZO fabric is produced in Faisalabad, Pakistan's primary textile manufacturing centre. Consolidating production in one location means tighter quality control, consistent spec enforcement, and faster turnaround on large orders.

Headquarters are in Lahore. Orders are dispatched to buyers across Lahore, Faisalabad, Karachi, and Peshawar. Minimum order: 10,000 linear metres per order, supplied in thaan/roll format.

Compliance

Yarn-Supplier Certifications

The following certifications are held at the yarn-supplier level — not at the ENZO entity level. This is the standard structure for Pakistani textile manufacturers sourcing certified yarn. Full documentation is available on request via info@enzolhr.com for procurement review.

OEKO-TEX Standard 100

Yarn-level testing for harmful substances across textile components. Covers the raw material inputs used in ENZO fabric production.

Better Cotton Initiative

Certified cotton grown with reduced water use and responsible soil management — applied at the yarn-supplier level to our cotton inputs.

Control Union

Independent third-party verification for quality and sustainability claims across our yarn supply chain.
