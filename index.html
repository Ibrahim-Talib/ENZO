About ENZO

Manufacturer Direct. No Intermediaries.

ENZO (Humayun Ibrahim Textile) has operated in wholesale fabric trade since 2014, with production centered in Faisalabad — Pakistan's primary textile manufacturing hub. We supply loom-to-buyer, with full traceability from production to delivery. Our clients are stockists, distributors, retailers, and fashion houses who need consistent quality, reliable restock cycles, and pricing that reflects a direct trade relationship — not an intermediary's margin.
