Manufacturing overview

Loom to delivery discipline.

Established in 2014, Humayun Ibrahim Textile operates ENZO as a B2B-only fabric label focused on Boski, Wash n Wear, Cotton, and seasonal ranges. Production is centered in Faisalabad, Pakistan's primary textile manufacturing capital, with consistent loom-to-delivery tracking.
